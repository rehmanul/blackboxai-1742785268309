Extract Data from Single URL

What is extracted?
Web Stryker extracts company information, contact details, and product data from websites. This includes company names, descriptions, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, product listings, prices, and more.
AI Enhancement
When enabled, AI enhancement improves extraction quality by using OpenAI and Knowledge Graph to categorize products, determine company types, and enrich extracted data with additional context.
Crawl Depth
Crawl depth determines how many links deep the extractor will follow. Higher values extract more data but take longer and consume more resources. Start with lower values for quicker results.
